Motivation

How does life look through the 'pleasure, passion and purpose' lens?

For me life becomes a lot simpler when looked at though this lens. It reminds me of a quote I heard a while a ago that resonated with me which goes something like: “A person needs three things to be happy: someone/something to love, something to do and something to look forward to.” I think the idea of ‘pleasure, passion and purpose’ is a more fundamental way of looking at same concept.

My core values (in no particular order) and what I stand for.

Productiveness and efficiency: something I learnt about myself a while ago is that for me to feel content at the end of the day/week/month I need to feel like I’ve achieved something. This can be at work, around the house or even learning a new skill / personal improvements. This directly relates to the purpose component in the 3 C’s.

Openness / Honesty: sometimes the truth isn’t the easiest route but it usually works out the best in the end.

Integrity: stand for what you believe in not just when it is convenient.

Equality / Fairness: this for me means that everyone should be given a fair go and an equal chance and also that everyone should contribute however they can.

What did you learn from your 'mindfulness conversation'? How did the other person find it?

I learnt just how much can be missed in a conversation due to wandering attention and misunderstanding.

They learnt how enjoyable it can to talk uninterrupted and with someone’s full attention.

Which of the three suggested techniques will you use to sustain your practice?

The walking meditation technique appeals to me the most as it can not only strengthen the mind but can exercise the body.

Any other musings?

The more of this book that I read the more the idea of meditation makes sense. The idea that you can train and exercise the ability to focus and bring your attention back to the task at hand makes a lot of sense but I’d never really considered it before.

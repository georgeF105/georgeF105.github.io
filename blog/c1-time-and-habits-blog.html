Which timebox and habits techniques did you try?

I originally tried using a one hour time box and set about to read the resource material but found an hour too long. Time boxes of around 20 - 25 minutes, like the Pomodoro Technique seems to work better for less engaging tasks.

What did you find interesting?

I found the concept of Flow very interesting as it helped explain some of the more enjoyable and productive work I have done in the past. Particularly the note in the Pick the Brain checklist around the fact that the task must be of the right level of difficulty, which explains why a difficult task after some practice can become more enjoyable and how a task can become tedious over time.

What worked for you?

I found breaking the task up into smaller time periods with mini breaks made completing the task and staying focused easier.

Which will you experiment further with?

I like the idea of informal meditation, I currently work at a job that takes about half an hour to drive to. This is (usually) a good time to relax before and after work. This is a time that I would miss and would be keen to try and replicate if lost.
